$$ f(x) = y $$

$$ f_2(f_1(x)) = y $$

$$ f_2(f_1(x_1, x_2)) = (y_1, y_2) $$

$$ Y(f_1)(x_1) = f_1(x_1) = y_1 $$

$$ Y(f_1, f_2, \ldots, f_l)(x_1, x_2, \ldots, x_i) = (y_1, y_2, \ldots, y_o) $$

<div class="text-center">
  l = layer, i = input, o = output
</div>
